<h2>Physical Book</h2>

<p>5 sheets of 8.5 X 11 inch blank white printer paper, folded in half.  Holes poked along spine every 1/2 inch using a thumb tack.  Spine sewn with a needle and thread, tied with many overhand knots at both ends.  Book has form factor of a "zine".  Combines with other physical booklets to be a full Tree Booklet in the Path of Watershed/Geometron.
</p>
