MBK Arduino Learning Kit

Kit Bill of Materials

  • MBK Oscillator Shield Board
  • Arduino UNO board
  • USB A/B printer cable
  • cheap bullet vibrator
  • Laptop or desktop computer(any OS)
  • Wire cutters/strippers
  • Volt meter to determine polarity of wires to vibrator(or LED)
